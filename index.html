Virginia Tech Climbing Club

The climbing club at Virginia Tech is a student organization dedicated to helping people of all skill levels experience the joy of climbing. The club provides opportunities to meet other climbers, slide shows to see what other people are climbing, clinics to demonstrate some of the skills climbers need, and organizes trips to get out on the rock. Club meetings are open to anyone who would like to come. If you have any questions about the club you can e-mail any of the club officers and they will be glad to help you.

Listserv

If you're not familiar with listservs any e-mail sent to the listserv will be received by everyone subscribed to the listserv. The club's listserv can be used to look for climbing partners, ask questions, or make announcements of interest to the general climbing community.

If you wish to send an e-mail to the listserv send it to VTclimbing@listserv.vt.edu

If you want to subscribe to the listserv send an e-mail to listserv@listserv.vt.edu. Leave the subject blank. In the body type "subscribe vtclimbing" followed by your first name and last name. You will receive an e-mail from listserv@listserv.vt.edu. Follow the directions in the e-mail and you will be added to the listserv. You should receive a confirmation e-mail once you're done.

If you want to unsubscribe from the listserv send an e-mail to listserv@listserv.vt.edu
Leave the subject blank. In the body type "SIGNOFF vtclimbing" .

Membership

Benefits of Membership: $8 day pass at Rise Up Climbing Gym with valid Rock Climbing Club Membership Card. 10% off purchases at Back Country in Christiansburg, VA . 10% off gear rentals at Venture Out in Squires Student Center.
